The story of an Arborist who decided to become a Web Developer

Introduction

This is a story of a fellow named Feter Priesen... It starts at the the age of 14.

Feter grew up in the mountains in a place called the High Grounds.

It was here he learned at the young age of 14 to operate a chain saw.

He started working for neighbours after school and on weekends cutting firewood to make money.

One day the client he was working for hired a tree climber to limb up a few trees in the garden to allow for some more light.

It was that day and moment Feter knew what he wanted to do!

Feter was amazed with the fact that this guy was getting paid to climb trees?!

Chapter 1

This is chapter 1. When Feter turned 18 he was hired by a tree service as grounds man. It was only a few short months before he was being trained to climb. Within the next year Feter kept excelling at his job and was a foreman and running a crew within a year.


After a couple years he went to chicago to buy a red bucket truck, and a yellow chipper.

He named his company Top Notch Tree Service ©
