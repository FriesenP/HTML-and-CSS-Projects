Border Collies

Introduction

This is a story of a Border Collie named Lua.

Lua is 12 years old and is a red and white collie! She is from Idaho and her parents are cattle herders. Lua has very good manners and is super smart!

Lua tried doing some herding when she was younger. She started with sheep and it didnt go very well! She ended up getting circled by the sheep! The only border collie to get herded, so that was the end of that!

Chapter 1

This is chapter 1. When Lua decided she didn't like herding, she decided to try agility and flyball. She was the fastest dog there!
